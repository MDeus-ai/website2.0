I am a mathematical researcher and systems engineer with a BSc in Statistics from Kyambogo University. My work focuses on the intersection of mathematical statistics and hardware-level execution. I am currently a Research Intern at the Infectious Diseases Institute (IDI) Mulago, where I apply rigorous statistical modeling to complex epidemiological datasets. My technical focus is on bypassing high-level abstractions to understand the physics of computation translating statistical algorithms into performant C++, JAX, and CUDA.
